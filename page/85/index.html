Wireless Before There Was Wireless

This thing is neat if you live in 1999. I believe it was a Woot pur­chase, and came in handy when a first-gen XBox needed a con­nec­tion three walls and a floor away from the router.

It’s a point-to-point wire­less eth­er­net con­nec­tion. Fuck if I know the real name, but that’s what I’m call­ing it. Got that Logitech?

All you do is plug in one box to an eth­er­net port on your router, and the other box to the device you wish to use. Use­ful for items that can’t con­nect to the wire­less net­works those darn kids use these days.
