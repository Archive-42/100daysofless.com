The 100 Days of Less Fake FAQ

Con­sid­er­ing this site has only been active for a cou­ple days, I haven’t had to field many ques­tions from “read­ers.” That I believe a few issues need to be addressed is more a reflec­tion of inter­nal dia­logue than any flesh-and-blood inter­ro­ga­tion. This post could have just as eas­ily been called, “Exam­in­ing Worry & Self-Doubt.”

Here’s to hop­ing I cut some real ques­tions off at the pass.

When do you start?
I tried to make the mas­sive cal­en­dar at the top of the page as intu­itive as pos­si­ble, but maybe I fucked that up. Novem­ber 21st is Day 1, and from that point on you’ll be able to nav­i­gate each entry through the calendar.
What do you mean by get­ting rid of “almost” everything?
Fuck, I can’t get rid of every­thing. I’m sure those around me will appre­ci­ate it if I hang on to a spare pair of clean Bat­man undies. Exactly how much shit does “almost” cover? I want to fit what’s left in a 20L back­pack 32L back­pack.
What are you going to do with the site when you’re done?
I don’t know. Let’s say we take it one step at a time, buddy.
You’re just going to tell us about your…stuff?
Hell no, I’m going to make you look at it too.
Hey, what if I want some of your junk?
I’m work­ing on a process for mak­ing the shit I’m going to trash avail­able for res­cue. Expect a post with details tomor­row or Friday.

If you hap­pen to have any real ques­tions please post a com­ment, send me a mes­sage on Twit­ter, or shoot me an e-mail.
