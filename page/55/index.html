Plastic Drawer Two

The next drawer down the plas­tic dorm-room remnant:

Items up for grabs are again marked by an asterisk (*).

  • A shitty check­book cover.*
  • A car charger for some unknown old phone. I sus­pect one of those Nokia bricks.*
  • Another local used book store receipt.
  • The busi­ness card for one very nice pri­vate dancer in the Santa Bar­bara area, Piper. I can­not promise she still works.*
  • Cal Poly CPE 480 course material.*
  • A bro­ken lightbulb.
  • Pro­tec­tive glasses for chem lab or some kind of shop.*
  • A bag con­tain­ing var­i­ous pins and patches for ath­letic and scholas­tic achieve­ments (All-League Golf, wooooo) as well as one 20-sided dice. Yes I’m a dork.
  • A small col­lec­tion of pho­tos from my youth. My mom gave them to me when I left for col­lege and they bring back some fan­tas­tic mem­o­ries. These are exactly the kinds of items that prove extremely tough to let go. But they must. Mom, before you com­ment a storm up, yes I will save them until I am home and give them back to you, if you wish. Don’t worry.
