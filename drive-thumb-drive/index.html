Drive Thumb, Drive

Hur­ray for SXSW 2008 swag. How­ever, con­sid­er­ing this thing is smaller than a CD in both size and space (see the sticker for capac­ity info), and I have an 8GB already, there’s no need to keep it.

Don’t have a big­ger USB drive, a free Drop­box account*, or Inter­net access (how the hell are you read­ing this?!) This lit­tle guy can be all yours.

* Yes, FTC. If peo­ple sign up for Drop­box through my link I get a lit­tle extra space for free. Eat it.
